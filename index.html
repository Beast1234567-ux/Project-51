Some Interesting Facts About camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.
  • Camels can travel at up to 40 miles per hour – the same as a racehorse!

Some Interesting Facts About pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.
  • Pigeons can fly at altitudes up to and beyond 6000 feet, and at an average speed of 77.6 mph.

Some Interesting Facts About Panda

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink(as babies) to white and black.
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Some Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons are that are a part of Inguana Suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a Prehensile Tale that they use to wrap around the tree branches
